Expertise in Every Frame

My journey as a content creator is defined by a deep-seated commitment to quality and truth. Rooted in nearly two decades of community activism, I use digital storytelling to bridge the divide between cutting-edge technology and the Cambodian public.

Beyond visual aesthetics, I specialize in navigating the complex digital landscape to produce high-impact infographics and videos. My work turns everyday digital challenges into opportunities for collective education, establishing content creation as a pillar for nationwide empowerment.

Bridging the Digital Divide

I am a long-term Khmer localization contributor with a strong focus on translation, linguistic review, and community-driven open-source projects. Since 2020, I have actively contributed to international translation and localization communities through Mozilla Pontoon, Crowdin, and Localization Lab.

My role goes beyond translation—I regularly serve as a Reviewer, ensuring linguistic quality, terminology consistency, and cultural appropriateness for Khmer users. My contributions support digital inclusion, internet freedom, and online safety.
