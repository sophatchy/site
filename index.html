About Me

Originally from Banteay Meanchey, I have been actively involved in youth and social activities since 2006. Over the last decade, I have transitioned from a curious student to a key figure in Cambodia's tech ecosystem.

Currently, I specialize in Digital Literacy and Internet Safety. My mission is to ensure that as Cambodia goes digital, its people stay safe, informed, and creative.
